Series Timeline

  • Episode 1 – The Revival of the Literary Club

    "Oreki reluctantly joins the Classic Literature Club and helps solve the mystery of the locked clubroom. Chitanda’s curiosity sparks the investigation. Oreki uses logic to uncover the truth behind the locked room."

    Release Date: April 22, 2012
    Arc: Introduction
    Mystery: Why the clubroom was locked.
    Quote: “If I don’t have to do it, I won’t. If I have to, I’ll do it quickly.” – Oreki

  • Episodes (2–4) – The Past of Hyouka

    "The club investigates the origins of the "Hyouka" anthology and Chitanda’s uncle’s connection to it. They uncover long-forgotten aspects of her family history. The investigation reveals hidden details of Chitanda’s uncle's past."

    Release Date: April 29 – May 13, 2012
    Arc: Hyouka Arc
    Mystery: The story behind “Hyouka” and Chitanda’s uncle.
    Quote: “Watashi, kininarimasu!” – Chitanda

  • Episodes (5–10) – The Movie Mystery

    "The club unravels the mystery behind an unfinished student film and its intended ending. Hidden motivations come to light, leading to a confrontation with Irisu. Oreki uses his sharp reasoning to uncover the film's true culprit."

    Release Date: May 20 – June 24, 2012
    Arc: Kudryavka’s Order / Movie Arc
    Mystery: Who the film's intended culprit was.
    Quote: “You were chosen, not because you're clever, but because you're obedient.” – Irisu

  • Episodes (11–16) – Kanya Festival Arc

    "The club investigates a series of thefts during the school’s cultural festival. They work to uncover the identity of the thief and the method behind the thefts. The case reveals deeper emotional conflicts within the group."

    Release Date: July 1 – August 5, 2012
    Arc: School Festival Mystery
    Mystery: The identity and method of the thief.
    Quote: “Don’t let it get to you… even if someone else did it better.” – Satoshi

  • Episode 17 – The Doll That Took a Detour

    "Oreki and Chitanda investigate the disappearance of a puppet during a shrine parade. The investigation reveals the puppet’s hidden significance. The case draws Oreki in as he realizes the personal stakes behind it."

    Release Date: August 12, 2012
    Arc: Standalone Mystery
    Mystery: Why the puppet was hidden and by whom.
    Quote: “It’s not about the mystery, it’s about who needs the answer.” – Oreki

  • Episodes 18–19 – Confession Arc

    "Oreki struggles to understand Chitanda’s subtle emotions. The episodes explore Oreki’s feelings toward her and her emotional growth. Oreki gains a deeper understanding of Chitanda's feelings and motivations."

    Release Date: August 19 – August 26, 2012
    Arc: Emotional Growth
    Mystery: The meaning behind Chitanda’s actions.
    Quote: “Would you have done it if I hadn’t asked?” – Chitanda

  • Episodes 20–21 – The Antique Study

    "The club uncovers a past mistake by a teacher in an entrance exam. They learn about the teacher’s decision that led to a wrong question being included. The investigation unearths an emotional story from the teacher’s past."

    Release Date: September 2 – September 9, 2012
    Arc: Hidden Truths
    Mystery: Why the wrong question was included in the test.
    Quote: “He may have been wrong, but he wasn’t lazy.” – Oreki

  • Episode 22 – The Distant Festival

    "Oreki and Chitanda walk together under cherry blossoms, reflecting on their relationship. The episode explores their emotional growth and their uncertain future. It provides closure to their journey together."

    Release Date: September 16, 2012
    Arc: Epilogue
    Mystery: None — emotional character development.
    Quote: “There are things I want to protect, even if I don't understand them yet.” – Chitanda
